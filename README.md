## 🛡️ Take Back Control of Your Browsing
BlockAds is a fast, lightweight, and incredibly secure ad-blocking application for Android. It runs entirely on your device using a local VPN connection, ensuring that your data never leaves your phone while blocking annoying ads, trackers, and malicious websites.

## ✨ Features
* **Zero-Setup Ad Blocking**: Just tap "Connect" and enjoy an ad-free web.
* **Pause When You Need To**: Need to access a blocked site temporarily? Pause the blocker for a customized amount of time, and it will automatically resume.
* **100% Local Privacy**: The VPN is entirely local. We don't route your traffic to external servers or collect your data.
* **Beautiful Material You Design**: A gorgeous, modern interface with seamless Light and Dark mode support that adapts to your system.
* **Multilingual**: Supports English, Hebrew, Arabic, French, German, and Spanish out of the box.

## 🚀 How It Works
Instead of requiring complicated proxy setups or root access, BlockAds utilizes Android's built-in VPN functionality. It creates a "local" VPN on your device that intercepts Domain Name System (DNS) requests. When an app tries to connect to a known ad or tracker domain, BlockAds instantly blocks the connection before it can even load!

## 📦 Installation
1. Go to the **Releases** page on GitHub.
2. Download the latest `BlockAds-release.apk`.
3. Open the downloaded file to install it on your Android device.
